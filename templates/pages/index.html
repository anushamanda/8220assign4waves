Omaha FireFighters Wellness System


The Omaha FireFighters Wellness System is the website created with
the purpose to make the lives of our hero easy.
We all know that with the help of technology, our routine can be
improved, so this system will help the Omaha Firefighters to download and
upload their forms regarding their injuries, and will communicate with their
supervisors.


In Omaha, there are in total of 650 firefighter who face many hazardous and
dangerous situations in order to save people. It is very important to
maintain their health records. Omaha Fire department wellness system will
help to maintain and retrieve these health records in a simple way. 
Firefighter can login to the website and download PDF forms as per the
injury occurred. They can manually fill the record and upload it to the
website in the form of PDF, TIFF or JPG. Superior/admin will login and
check for the uploaded files and will approve or disapprove. When he
disapproves, he will comment the reason for the disapproval and will send
the comment to the firefighter to rectify. Upon approval, the file will
be stores in the system and will allow supervisor to decide the condition
of the firefighter.
